Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make changes to my landing page after its finished?

Each pricing package includes customer support after your mobile landing page is finished. This includes changing any links, updating screenshots, or adding page sections. Any additions outside the pricing template that you have purchased, may require you to increase your monthly fee.

How can your service provide a landing page so quickly?

We have perfected our design and development processes and created a landing page template which we use for all our customers, but will be customized to fit the design and color scheme of your application.

Are you sure I need a mobile app landing page?

Yes! Research has shown that having a mobile app landing page not only allows you to increase the amount of channels for which to acquire new customers, but also leads to an increase in app downloads!

Doesn't the Google Play Store and IOS App Store create a landing page for me?

While the Play Store and App Store display information and screenshots for your app, it requires customers to first search for and select your app from thousands of others just like it. A landing page hosted on a custom domain allows you to drive your customers to your play store or app store page.
